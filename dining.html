Dining in AlUla

Taste the
Flavours of AlUla

AlUla is quickly becoming a melting pot of the Saudi culinary scene. Found at the crossroads of old and new, it’s a bourgeoning foodie paradise. Historically known for its citrus, lush farmlands provide a bounty of fresh produce – creating a gastronomic playground of inspiring ingredients for both international and local chefs.

Explore fine dining, causal eats and tried and true local spots that make AlUla unique.

Fine Dining

Multi-course tasting menus, delicate plates and bold flavours adorn the tables of AlUla’s finest restaurants. World-renowned chefs play with farm-fresh ingredients in a multitude of styles, blending old-world spices into inventive new dishes.

Quick and Casual

Quick and casual doesn’t skimp on taste and freshness. Think artisan pizza with a view or a vibrant knot of food trucks. AlUla is full of cafes for an afternoon pick-me-up and laid-back spots for when hunger hits.

Local Favourites

The secret is out on these local spots, but the queues speak for themselves. Perfectly busy, so you know they’re well-tested – don’t be surprised when you’re too late for the lamb or you’re seated Saudi style.

Adventure
AlUla

AlUla is Saudi Arabia’s flagship tourist destination, with events and festivals year-round. Disconnect and unwind at the AlUla Wellness Festival; discover long-ago kings and queens at the Ancient Kingdoms Festival; celebrate AlUla’s rich cultural heritage at Winter at Tantora; and explore the unspoiled skies, day and night, at the AlUla Skies Festival.
