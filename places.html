Explore the Heart of AlUla

Saudi Arabia is truly one of the last undiscovered places on earth. Recently opening up to the world, it is more than ready to share its unique culture with the world.

AlUla sits significantly on the ancient Incense Road, a magical oasis for weary travellers, pilgrims and locals alike. Many kingdoms once called AlUla home, leaving traces of their way of life, inscribing their stories into the sandstone rockfaces and carving intricate tombs among the mountains.

As hosts of this historical hub, the people of AlUla have a tremendous culture of hospitality seemingly seeped into their souls.

The last great adventure awaits in AlUla.

AlUla

AlUla is Saudi Arabia’s flagship tourist destination, with events and festivals year-round. Disconnect and unwind at the AlUla Wellness Festival; discover long-ago kings and queens at the Ancient Kingdoms Festival; celebrate AlUla’s rich cultural heritage at Winter at Tantora; and explore the unspoiled skies, day and night, at the AlUla Skies Festival.
