Understanding the Map of AlUla

The city of AlUla sits in a valley with tall mountains protecting the lush oasis. Because of this, the town itself is very long and skinny, and places like Hegra, Elephant Rock and Harrat Viewpoint are spread out throughout the surrounding desert.

However, there is a beating heart of AlUla – the perfect triad of the historic AlUla Old Town, the newer district of AlJadidah, and the flourishing AlUla Oasis. Pedestrian-friendly and all within walking distance of each other, each has its own distinct flavour.

To get the most out of your visit, it helps to understand where and what things are –
especially in AlUla.

At the Heart of AlUla

AlUla Old Town

While the area of the historic old town is quite sprawling, the designated AlUla Old Town is one main walking road lined with shops, restaurants, cafes and an amphitheatre. A twisting alleyway across from the amphitheatre takes you down to the entrance of the old fort, private parking and a sort-of way to get down to the AlUla Oasis.

Learn more

AlJadidah

Connected to AlUla Old Town by a community-painted “urban carpet,” AlJadidah is bursting with hang-out spots. Quirky hanging lamps, the hipster Cinema ElHoush, an art gallery, a retro roller rink, food trucks, and more can be found in this vibrant hub. With most things opening after 4 pm, visit at night for the live music, shisha and great vibes.

Learn more

AlUla Oasis

An area still undefined by paved roads, there is much to discover in the AlUla Oasis. A new extension from AlJadidah has opened up the AlUla Oasis, creating cute tea shops and towering palm swings. Explore further along the Heritage Oasis Trail for a close-up of the old mudbrick houses and farms all the way to Dadan.

Learn more

AlManshiyah Plaza

The secret is out on these local spots, but the queues speak for themselves. Perfectly busy, so you know they’re legit – don’t be surprised when you’re too late for the lamb or you’re seated Saudi style.

Elephant Rock

Quick and casual doesn’t skimp on taste and freshness. Think artisan pizza with a view or a vibrant knot of food trucks. AlUla is full of cafes for an afternoon pick-me-up and laid-back spots for when hunger hits.

AlUla

AlUla is Saudi Arabia’s flagship tourist destination, with events and festivals year-round. Disconnect and unwind at the AlUla Wellness Festival; discover long-ago kings and queens at the Ancient Kingdoms Festival; celebrate AlUla’s rich cultural heritage at Winter at Tantora; and explore the unspoiled skies, day and night, at the AlUla Skies Festival.
